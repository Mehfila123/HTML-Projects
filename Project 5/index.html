Runtime: 152 minutes
Budget: $125 million

Plot Summary

The film introduces Harry Potter, an orphaned boy who discovers on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard. Living with his neglectful aunt, uncle, and cousin, Harry’s life changes when he receives an acceptance letter to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

At Hogwarts, Harry befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, learns about his parents' tragic death at the hands of the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, and uncovers the existence of the Philosopher’s Stone (Sorcerer’s Stone in the U.S.), a magical artifact that grants immortality.

Harry, Ron, and Hermione work together to stop Voldemort from returning to power by obtaining the stone. The trio faces a series of challenges, including a giant three-headed dog, deadly plants, a chess game with life-sized pieces, and magical puzzles. In the climax, Harry confronts Professor Quirrell, who is revealed to be harboring Voldemort. Harry prevents Voldemort from claiming the stone, which is ultimately destroyed.

The movie ends with Harry returning to the Dursleys for the summer, anticipating another year at Hogwarts.
